Culture & Heritage

Boudhanath Stupa

One of the largest spherical stupas in Nepal and a UNESCO World Heritage Site, this ancient monument is the focal point of Tibetan Buddhism in Nepal.

Pashupatinath Temple

This sacred Hindu temple complex on the banks of the Bagmati River is one of the most important Shiva temples in the world.

Bhaktapur Durbar Square

Step back in time in this well-preserved ancient city known for its exquisite architecture and traditional Newari culture.
